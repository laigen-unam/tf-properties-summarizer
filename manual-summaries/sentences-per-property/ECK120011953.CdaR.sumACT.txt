Carbohydrate diacid regulator , CdaR , or sugar diacid regulator , SdaR , regulates genes involved in  uptake and metabolism of galactarate and glucarate  , and  it  is also autoregulated   . 
 SdaR responds to d-galactarate and d-glucarate , and even better to d-glycerate to activate genes it regulates  .
