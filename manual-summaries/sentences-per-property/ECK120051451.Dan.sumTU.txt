 Expression of ttdR -LRB- dan gene -RRB- is positively autoregulated in response to L-tartrate  and  activated by two-component system DcuSR in presence of L-tartrate , meso-tartrate , D-malate or fumarate . 
ttdR -LRB- dan gene -RRB- is repressed by FNR and nitrate and nitrite two-component systems NarXL and NarPQ  .
