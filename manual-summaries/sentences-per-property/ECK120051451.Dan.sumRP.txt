DNA-binding protein under anaerobic conditions , Dan , formerly TtdR or YgiP ,  is an LysR-type transcriptional regulator  for  L-tartrate fermentation  . 
 In presence of L - and meso-tartrate , Dan stimulates expression of divergently transcribed ttdABT operon  , encoding  L-tartrate dehydratase and L-tartrate : succinate antiporter  .
