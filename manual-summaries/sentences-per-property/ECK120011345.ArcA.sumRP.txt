ArcA represses operons involved in  respiratory metabolism , including those that encode products such as the tricarboxylic acid cycle enzymes , enzymes of the glyoxylate shunt , and enzymes of the pathway for fatty acid degradation . 
In addition , ArcA acts as a repressor for rpoS transcription . 
ArcA also activates a few operons encoding proteins involved in fermentative metabolism  .
